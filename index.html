About Me

Hello, I am a level designer with a years of experience. While working on personal projects I am also working on larger games and currently working on a game called Zero Space. I am also working on smaller games with more of a focus on level designing, narrative writing, and lighting.

I like to focus on set dressing and white boxing. I have also led teams and helped out other leads with distributing tasks within groups.
